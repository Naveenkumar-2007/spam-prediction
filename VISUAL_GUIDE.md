## 🎨 Visual Design Principles Used

1. **Card Design** - Clean, elevated containers
2. **Gradients** - Modern purple-to-pink hero
3. **Icons** - Font Awesome throughout
4. **Spacing** - Generous padding and margins
5. **Typography** - Clear hierarchy
6. **Colors** - Meaningful (green=safe, red=danger)
7. **Animations** - Smooth and purposeful
8. **Shadows** - Depth and elevation
9. **Responsive** - Mobile-first approach
10. **Accessibility** - Clear labels and contrast
